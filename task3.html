About Justin Bieber:

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and musician who gained international recognition in the 2000s. Born on March 1, 1994, he rose to fame in the digital age by posting his videos on YouTube. Bieber achieved significant success at a young age with hits like "Baby," "Sorry," and "Love Yourself," solidifying his popularity worldwide. Throughout his career, he has faced various challenges and demands, but he has managed to overcome them and remain a prominent figure in the music industry.
